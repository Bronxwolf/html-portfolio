The Best Movies According to Damien

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Little Shop of Horrors

This is my absolute favorite movie of all time. I know every word to every song.

Labryinth

Its very similar to Little Shop of Horrors. Except there is way more fantasy and over the top animation.

New Jack City

Very different than my top 2, but it is the epitome of what a time based movie should be.
